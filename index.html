We have no official portfolio for these games and they are not published at the moment. This is just a quick page with videos and short descriptions of our casual games. Most games are not yet polished, but included here in case you saw some potential in them. Maybe some of them could be considered for publishing / testing with you

Soda Rescue
"Use your finger to dig through the delicious landscape and rescue all the tasty Soda and delicious candy and doughnuts". Has endless levels and progressive difficulty. Levels have physics-based elements and traps. Should be quite addictive


Build More (Blocky game visuals)
Idle arcade where you manufacture and ship different products. This version has blocky, outlined graphics


Build More (Mall-version, game name not final)
Idle arcade where you manufacture, package and ship different products. This version has more casual style graphics and a mall you deliver the products. Not 100% finished or ready to store


Volcano!
Dig the ground and control lava flow to destroy cities and stuff. Three different worlds (themes) to burn. Endless levels


Block Masters
Based on the popular block puzzle with Tetris-blocks. Games are player vs player-matches. Not 100% finished or ready to store


Casual Art / Casual Crafts
Mix paints and paint pictures and sell them to customers. Also has a version where you start with wood (with bark), sand it, paint it, cut the picture out of the wood (asmr). Not 100% finished or ready to store



City Raid
Dig the ground and control soldiers through cities and blasting buildings and shooting enemies with machine guns, rocket launchers, flame throwers, hand grenades. Use specials like airstrikes and nukes to destroy bosses


Paint Shop
Idle arcade where you paint vehicles
